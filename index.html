About Me

I was a young girl who loved talking to people, asking questions, and hearing their stories. I would do my own talk show with friends where I would be the host and they would take on a character or celebrity identity, and I would interview them, talk about news, and the weather. I loved it!

Even though I tended to be quiet in the classroom through my school years, I was comfortable to have a voice and be heard when it came time for a speech. I knew then, as I know now, this is my gift. I also knew when the time was right that I would use this gift for something good and with an admirable purpose.

For many years, I filmed events and captured the memories and happiness of many people. I finally decided that it was time to move from behind the camera back into the front to use my voice to share stories, inspire, and help others find happiness.

My mission is to get people motivated to pursue a dream, do what they love, be passionate about life, and to help as many people as I can find their inner smile and truly be happy.
